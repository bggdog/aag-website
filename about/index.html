/ About AAG

The team behind the back office.

Aurora Advisory Group is a back-office outsourcing firm built for growth-stage operators in healthcare and professional services. We handle the operational infrastructure that slows businesses down — so the people who built them can focus on what they’re actually good at.

“We do what no one wants to do — so you can do what you were meant to.”

Our story

AAG was built around a simple observation: most growth-stage businesses hit a point where the back office becomes a real problem, but they’re not yet at the size where hiring a full team makes financial sense. They end up in a messy middle — doing too much themselves, or piecing together solutions that don’t quite work.

We exist for that moment. We step in as the operational infrastructure layer — handling billing, compliance, accounting, credentialing, and more — at a fraction of the cost of building it in-house and without the management overhead of a full internal team.

Our clients are practices, clinics, and professional services firms that are growing fast enough to need real back-office support but aren’t ready to hire eight specialists to cover eight different functions. We cover all of it, scoped exactly to what’s needed, with no long-term contracts.
